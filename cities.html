Cities spend billions building and maintaining parks and recreation facilities — but programming has always been the hard part. Sandlot gives you the programming infrastructure without the overhead, the staff, or the storage.

🏟️

Big Facilities, Limited Reach

Your recreation centers serve the neighborhoods within driving distance. The other 80% of your city — apartment complexes, smaller parks, underserved ZIP codes — gets nothing. Sandlot fills that gap.

👷

Staffing Is the Bottleneck

Hiring, training, and retaining qualified recreation staff is the hardest problem in municipal parks. Sandlot brings fully trained, certified Game Managers to every event. No city HR involvement required.

📦

Equipment Storage & Maintenance

City recreation equipment ages, breaks, gets stolen, and requires storage space. Sandlot owns all equipment. We maintain it, store it, and replace it on our dime. Zero burden on city facilities.

📊

Programming ROI Is Hard to Measure

City councils want data. How many residents were served? Which neighborhoods? Which age groups? Sandlot provides monthly participation reports, ZIP-code breakdowns, and constituent satisfaction data — ready for your council presentation.

How We Operate
TURNKEY FROM
DAY ONE.
01

We Map Your City

Our team works with Parks & Rec to identify optimal locations — underserved parks, high-density neighborhoods, school grounds, and plazas. We build a rotating schedule that maximizes geographic coverage.

02

Contract Execution

We provide an RFP-ready proposal, insurance certificates (GL + commercial auto), and a standard city services agreement. Our legal templates are already written for municipal procurement. Approval-to-launch in 30–60 days.

Sandlot trucks arrive on schedule. Our AI routes trucks optimally across your park system daily. Game Managers run sessions for all ages — from kids' flag football to senior pickleball to family volleyball nights.
