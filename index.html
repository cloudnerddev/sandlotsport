The Process
HOW SANDLOT
WORKS

No facilities to book. No equipment to buy. No league to join. We handle everything — you just show up and play.

01

Sign Up & Pick Your Sport

Join Sandlot and browse upcoming events near you. Pick a sport, reserve your spot, and invite friends. All through the app.

02

We Come to You

A fully-loaded Sandlot truck arrives at your location with all equipment, a certified Game Manager, and everything needed to run a world-class session.

03

Play. Level Up. Repeat.

Track your sessions, earn badges, compete on leaderboards, and explore new sports. Your membership keeps you on the field all year.

04

Get Rewarded

After every session, unlock exclusive gear offers and discounts from top sports brands. The more you play, the better the deals.
