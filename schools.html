The Research
SPORTS AREN'T
OPTIONAL.
THEY'RE ACADEMIC.

The link between physical activity and academic performance is among the most robust in education research. Sandlot isn't just a fun add-on — it's a documented academic support strategy.

🧠

Academic Performance

Students who participate in physical activity show measurable improvements in attention, memory, and academic achievement. PE isn't a break from learning — it enables it.

❤️

Mental Health Outcomes

Youth athletes show lower rates of anxiety, depression, and behavioral issues. With youth mental health at a national crisis point, regular physical activity is one of the most evidence-based interventions available.

🤝

Social-Emotional Skills

Teamwork, resilience, leadership, and conflict resolution — the skills most employers cite as critical — are all built on the playing field. Sandlot is SEL programming that doesn't feel like a program.

🏅

Equity in Access

Students from low-income families are half as likely to participate in sports. Sandlot comes to the school — eliminating the transportation, equipment cost, and fee barriers that keep kids on the sidelines.
